# Internal / Backlink Rules

Corresponds to the flowchart step "list which sections can carry links."

## Internal Links (hub-and-spoke)

- Each spoke article links up to its parent "pillar page" (the 5 pillars).
- Link to 2–3 related articles in the same cluster (lateral linking).
- BOFU / decision-stage sections link to `/consult` (B2B discovery) or the corresponding lead magnet.
- Use natural-language anchor text built from the target keyword; do not keyword-stuff.
- No orphan articles: every article is linked into by at least one pillar page.

## Backlinks / External Links (Search Everywhere)

- In sections that cite data / third-party perspectives, link out to authoritative sources (to build E-E-A-T).
- Flag the sections best suited for "off-site citation" → later restate them on Reddit/Threads/LinkedIn/Medium and run digital PR, generating the third-party mentions that AI engines value.
- Flagship articles should plan at least 1 off-site touchpoint.

## Output Format

| Section | Link Type | Target | Anchor Text |
|---|---|---|---|
| <which paragraph/subheading> | internal/external | <page/source> | <anchor text> |
