# Keyword Filtering SOP

Corresponds to the green node "Keyword Filtering SOP" in the flowchart. Filter first, then cluster.

## Exclusion Principles

- Terms unrelated to the brand's services (purely informational, no commercial relevance).
- Markets / audiences the brand explicitly does not target.
- Duplicate or excessively synonymous terms (merge into a head term + variants).
- Terms with search volume approaching 0 that are not strategic long-tail.

## Retention Priorities

- Terms that map to genuine persona pain points.
- BOFU decision-intent terms (consultant, ROI, comparison, recommendation) — the funnel goal.
- Terms that can be differentiated with Zynkr first-party evidence.
- AEO-friendly full-sentence question-style terms.

## Pre-Clustering Reminders

- Intent first, category second.
- A term may be temporarily assigned to multiple clusters; decide its primary assignment when splitting versions.
- Tag the language (zh-TW / flagship EN).
